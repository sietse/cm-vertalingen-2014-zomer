Motetten
--------

Let all mortal flesh keep silence 
=================================

music: E.C. Bairstow
words:

Let all mortal flesh keep silence and stand with fear and trembling, and lift itself above all earthly thought.
For the King of Kings and Lord of Lords, Christ our God,
cometh forth to be our oblation and to be giv’n for Food to the faithful.
Before him come the choirs of angels, with every principality and pow’r:
The cherubim with many eyes and winged seraphim, who veil their faces as they shout exultingly the hymn. Alleluia! 

Tenor + Bas: 
Let all mortal flesh keep silence and stand with fear and trembling. 


Justorum anime
==============

music: C.V. Stanford
words: Proverbs 3

Justorum animae in manu Dei sunt et non tanget illos tormentum malitiae. Visi sunt oculis insipientium mori, illi autem sunt in pace. 


Coelos ascendit hodie
=====================

music: C.V. Stanford
words: 

Coelos ascendit hodie, alleluia!
Jesus Christus Rex gloriae, alleluia!
Sedet ad Patris dexteram, alleluia!
Gubernat coelum et terram [etc.]
Iam finem habent omnia
Patris Davidis carmina
Iam Dominus cum Domino
Sedet in Dei solio
In hoc triumpho maximo
Benedicamus Domino
Laudatur Sancta Trinitas
Deo dicamus gratias
Amen.


Beati quorum via
================

music: C.V. Stanford
words: Psalm 119:1

Beati quorum via integra est, qui ambulant in lege Domini.
