Let all mortal flesh keep silence

:music: E.C. Bairstow :words:

Let all mortal flesh keep silence and stand with fear and trembling, and
  lift itself above all earthly thought.
For the King of Kings and Lord of Lords, Christ our God, cometh forth to
  be our oblation and to be giv’n for Food to the faithful.
Before him come the choirs of angels, with every principality and pow’r:
The cherubim with many eyes and winged seraphim, who veil their faces as
  they shout exultingly the hymn.
Alleluia!

Tenor + Bas:

Justorum animae

:music: C.V. Stanford :words: Proverbs 3

Justorum animae in manu Dei sunt et non tanget illos tormentum malitiae. Visi sunt oculis insipientium mori, illi autem sunt in pace.

Coelos ascendit hodie

:music: C.V. Stanford :words:

Coelos ascendit hodie, alleluia!
Jesus Christus Rex gloriae, alleluia!
Sedet ad Patris dexteram, alleluia!
Gubernat coelum et terram [etc.]
Iam finem habent omnia
Patris Davidis carmina
Iam Dominus cum Domino
Sedet in Dei solio
In hoc triumpho maximo
Benedicamus Domino
Laudatur Sancta Trinitas
Deo dicamus gratias
Amen.

Today Jesus Christ, the King of Glory, has ascended into the heavens,
He sits at the Father's right hand, ruling heaven and earth,
Now are David's songs fulfilled,
Now is the Lord with his Lord,
He sits upon the royal throne of God in this His greatest
Let us bless the Lord: let the Holy Trinity be praised,
Let us give thanks to the Lord,

Aujourd'hui, Jésus-Christ, le Roi de gloire est monté aux cieux,
Il est assis à la droite du Père et règne les cieux et la terre,
Les chansons de David ont été exaucées,
Le Seigneur est avec son Seigneur,
Il est assis sur le trône royal de Dieu, Alléluia!
Ceci est son grand triomphe, Alléluia!
Nous bénissons le Dieu:
Nous louons la Trinité Sainte,
Nous rendons grâces à Dieu, Alléluia! Amen.
